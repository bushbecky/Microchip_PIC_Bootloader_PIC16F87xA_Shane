***************************
How to compile this program
***************************

- Go to http://www.htsoft.com/
- Look under 'Free programs' and download Pacific C for DOS.
- Run \BIN\PPD.EXE, then use Make .. Load Project to load the .pjt file.
- Load downldr.c by selecting File..Open, then F10 to open downldr.c
- Turn off warnings by selecting Compile .. Stop on Warnings to OFF. This ignores the warnings. Dont
  worry about warnings, it works fine.
- Compile with F3 or Compile..Compile and Link.
